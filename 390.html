But as of now, I think we should do a hybrid system, as one or the other isn't perfect, but combining both would make our lives easier.

Whitelist - Only filters words that are “permitted”
Blocklist - Only filters words that are “not permitted”

Hybrid: Allows content that has purely whitelisted words, block entire content that contains a blocklisted word, and if the content has no matches for whitelist or blocklist make it a “for review” sort of ordeal
