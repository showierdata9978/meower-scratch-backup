The problem is rooted in the service we use to fetch IPs: https://www.ipify.org/. This service is blocked by numerous adblockers and also could be be block on computers and WIFI that are controlled by an admin restricting them. For example, my school blocked ipify and thus, I was not allowed to enter meower as the ip was reported blank. we simply can't allow blank IPs to access meower or that would be an exploit that malicious people could take advantage of.
