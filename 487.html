My flash drive didn't copy my private build of both the client and server, but fear not! I will try my best to release Beta 5 today.

As for the upcoming features within Beta 5:

1. A completely redesigned look and feel, with a focus on user experience. We're putting in more friendlier screens and simplified menus. Of course, you can always choose the old Meower layout.
2. Group and private chats. You can invite other Meower members to chats you create and quickly share ideas or collaborate in real-time.
3. Better safety features. We're putting all the stops in our moderation features. We'll be working around the clock to keep Meower safe.
4. Widescreen and mobile support. We've upgraded to a 16:9 aspect ratio and added support for touchscreen devices.
5. For developers, we're making Meower more open to 3rd party services and clients. You'll hear more about this feature later.

That's all for now. We're getting ready for Beta 5!
