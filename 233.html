Due to various development complications and setbacks, Meower will NOT be releasing on Scratch, rather, it will be initially released independently from Scratch. This will serve as a feature test and for debugging purposes only. It will be called “Public Beta Test 1”. This should not be mistaken for Yoom, a fork of Meower.

Features that I will try to implement in the test are:

1. Basic messaging functionality
2. Account authentication/profile page management
3. Discussion thread storage/management

The build of Meower, excluding these implemented tests, will remain vanilla and (hopefully) ST-compliant.

Also, I've been very busy working with my dad in our garage to finish a car for one of our customers. We're very busy, and our garage currently has no internet access (it's a quarter-mile down a hill from my nearest internet access). Because of this, I haven't been able to work on meower as much as I'd like to. I will try to start Public Test 1 when I wake up around noon today, EDT.
