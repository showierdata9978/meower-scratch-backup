Some updates!

1. I fixed some things related to CloudLink so Meower should now have a more sane login prompt.

And… for the news you all have been loathing.

I have concluded that publishing Meower on Scratch is too risky, so as soon as Beta 4.5 is complete, we will no longer be publishing builds of Meower on Scratch, indefinitely. We will be moving all development and releases onto Github or other self-managed solutions. There are too many issues going on with Scratch's management and I don't want Meower to get the ax by someone else's hands.

We will never give up! See you all soon.
